Description

Bronze Living Estate, situated in the tranquil Arapaja, Odo-Ona Kekere area of Ibadan, offers a luxurious living experience for those seeking a comfortable and modern lifestyle. The estate features 2 and 3 bedroom apartments with exquisite designs that exude elegance and style, promising to exceed your expectations of modern living.

Bronze Living Estate is equipped with estate facilities that cater to residents' needs, such as 24/7 power supply, uniformed security personnel, CCTV surveillance, street lights, and spacious parking space. These features ensure residents' safety and security, while also providing convenience and comfort.

The apartments at Bronze Living Estate offer spacious living areas, large windows for natural light, and modern amenities that cater to residents' needs. The estate's serene environment provides a peaceful and relaxing atmosphere, making it the perfect destination for individuals, couples, and families seeking a serene living environment.

Bronze Living Estate is conveniently located close to major commercial and social hubs, making it easy for residents to access essential services and amenities. With its exceptional features and amenities, Bronze Living Estate offers a luxurious and comfortable living experience that sets it apart from other residential complexes in Ibadan. If you are looking for a contemporary and luxurious living experience, Bronze Living Estate is the perfect destination for you.
